WorldcApp

As a huge fan of big football (some call it soccer) tournaments, I had to develop an app for the world cup in Brazil. Initially, I just wanted to have a place to see the results and standings. But friends and colleagues wanted to bet on the results, so I've added the possiblity for users to bet and to join groups where they could compete with each other.

For the first draft, I used AngularJS and FireBase. But when the world cup was approaching and I ran out of time, I switched to Meteor where I had more experience. The app is fully responsive and works on both widescreens and smartphones.

Website: worldcapp.eu

#meteor, #html, #css, #javascript, #jquery, #mongodb, #responsive

EasyInvoices

The easiest way to create and manage your invoices. EasyInvoices offers modern invoice designs but lets you use your own designs as well.

It feels like a native app but is built with web technologies (mainly Meteor). Though, the plan is to also bring it to mobile app stores using Cordova.

The backend for creating the invoices is made with Laravel.

You can register now for the closed beta version.

Website: easy-invoices.com

#meteor, #html, #css, #javascript, #jquery, #mongodb, #laravel, #responsive

Do you feel like you could organize your private or working life in a better way? Organizr can help you improving your organization.

OpenVDO

Using latest web technologies, OpenVDO is a videotelephony app that aims on privacy. It is being developed in collaboration with Cosmin Popescu (though, I am mainly working on the design part).

No need for signing up, no data is stored. The server is only used for the handshake between users, then it is a pure peer-to-peer connection.

Website: openvdo.eu

#webrtc, #websockets, #html, #css, #javascript, #jquery, #responsive
